Heavy metal doujin circle comprised of brothers 徳南(Tokunan) on guitar/bass/keyboard and 九宝時(Kyuuhouji) on drums.
Respectively, they are Masaru Teramae and Tadashi Teramae. They are brothers who frequently collaborate with each other, and formed this circle for Touhou music collaborations. Masaru is also a professional guitarist for Falcom jdk Band, which produces music and remixes for Nihon Falcom games, such as the Kiseki/Trails series and Ys.

Maintainer's short take:

A very intriguing album, and this time it's the classic Demetori but with a little bit of foreign melodic touch on top(track 1 and 5). Track 3 is honestly one of the best rock ballad songs I've heard in the recent years, and such a chaotic riffs and solos in track 4 reflects precisely the nature of Rumia's character and her theme, making this album a must-buy for me despite finishing rather short on the tracklist compared to their older works.
