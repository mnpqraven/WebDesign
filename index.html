What is this site about ?

This is and indexing site for doujinshi albums (同人).
Below are some recommended and highlight circles
Demetori
Heavy metal doujin circle comprised of brothers 徳南(Tokunan) on guitar/bass/keyboard and 九宝時(Kyuuhouji) on drums. Respectively, they are Masaru Teramae and Tadashi Teramae. They are brothers who frequently collaborate with each other, and formed this circle for Touhou music collaborations. Masaru is also a professional guitarist for Falcom jdk Band, which produces music and remixes for Nihon Falcom games, such as the Kiseki/Trails series and Ys.
